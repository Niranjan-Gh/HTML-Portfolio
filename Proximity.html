Proximity Of Virtue

Game Designer

Developed GDD, Mechanics, and Level Design for the Game.

Proximity of Virtue is a dark fantasy platformer set in a haunting, atmospheric world. With a rich, moody aesthetic, players must navigate treacherous environments, overcome deadly challenges, and ultimately face a powerful demon boss. The game blends precise platforming with a deep, immersive tone to deliver a compelling and intense experience.


Challenges Encounterd

Blending every element with the dark atmosphere was a creative challenge, but it was essential to bringing the game's vision to life.

Result

The finished game features responsive player controls, a shooting mechanic where the player fires orbs at enemies, and a quick dash ability for fast maneuvering.
