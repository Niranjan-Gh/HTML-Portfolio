Projects Worked on.

1.Proximity Of Virtue.

Proximity of Virtue is a dark fantasy platformer set in a haunting, atmospheric world. With a rich, moody aesthetic, players must navigate treacherous environments, overcome deadly challenges, and ultimately face a powerful demon boss. The game blends precise platforming with a deep, immersive tone to deliver a compelling and intense experience.


Learn more about Proximity of Virtue.

2.Train Jam.

Train Jam is a puzzle game where players guide passengers to the correct train coaches by matching their colors. The concept was inspired by Bus Jam, and I reimagined it with an Indian train aesthetic to give it a unique and culturally rich feel.


Learn more about Train Jam

3.Found at Terminal!.

This is a simple Puzzle/Time based game. The player works at the Terminal Lost & Found Center, tasked with returning lost items to their rightful owners. The goal is to quickly match items with their owners before time runs out, all while managing an increasing number of lost items.


Learn more about Found at Terminal!
