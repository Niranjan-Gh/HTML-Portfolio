Found At Terminal!.

Game Designer/Artist/UI/UX

Developed GDD, Mechanics, Assets and Level Design for the Game.

This is a simple Puzzle/Time based game. The player works at the Terminal Lost & Found Center, taskedwith returning lost items to their rightful owners. The goal istoquickly match items with their owners before time runs out, all while managing an increasing number of lost items.


Gameplay Loop

  1. Customers approach your desk looking for lost items.
  2. You must match luggage and objects to the correct person based on clues (color, tags, shape, etc.).
  3. As levels progress, the number of items increases, and customers become more impatient.

Result

The final result was a fast-paced and engaging game where players must manage lost items arriving on a conveyor belt and deliver them to the correct customers. The gameplay revolves around sorting and organizing items on a limited table space while staying alert for specific customer requests. A unique calling feature allows customers to inquire about particular items, and if the requested item appears on the conveyor belt, players must quickly identify and deliver it. This blend of time management and quick decision-making keeps the gameplay dynamic and rewarding.
